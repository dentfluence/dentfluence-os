Dentfluence — pricing packages

Value-based pricing: one dental-focused subscription replaces a ₹6,000–10,000/mo stack of generic CRM, accounting, marketing and messaging tools — while you keep control and cut staff dependency through automation. Elite is the premium flagship, offered selectively; ProConsult and modules serve the wider market cheaply. Elite plans add a one-time onboarding fee (₹15,000–25,000). All prices in ₹; annual ≈ 2 months free.
